About Me

Greetings! I'm a versatile artist with a passion for both animation and software engineering. While my background primarily lies in animation and 3D modeling, I'm currently exploring the realm of software development and code creation. As part of my journey, I've enrolled in an online bootcamp called CodeCademy, where I'm immersing myself in the basics of full stack development, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

In addition to expanding my coding skills, I'm actively seeking apprenticeships and junior roles in software development, software engineering, or full stack development within the tech industry. I'm excited about the prospect of merging my artistic sensibilities with programming expertise to create immersive and interactive experiences.

Throughout my artistic development, I've honed my skills in various software tools. Alongside Maya, Blender, zBrush, Substance 3D Painter, and the Adobe Suite (including Flash/Animate, Photoshop, After Effects, and Premiere Pro), I'm now familiarizing myself with programming languages and frameworks that empower me to bring my artistic visions to life through interactive digital solutions.

While fantasy holds a special place in my heart, I'm constantly seeking growth and pushing the boundaries of my craft. Crafting fantastical creatures and character modeling remain my passions, but now I'm eager to employ my artistic and technical skills in the world of software development. I take great pride in delivering captivating artwork and engaging experiences that transport viewers to enchanting worlds.

Collaboration is an integral part of my creative process. I enjoy exchanging ideas with fellow artists and developers, combining our expertise to produce extraordinary results. Whether it's through visual storytelling or interactive applications, I strive to captivate and engage audiences by delivering top-quality work.

To summarize, my artistic journey has led me from 2D art to the dynamic world of 3D, and now I'm expanding my horizons into the realm of software development. With a strong foundation in 3D sculpting, modeling, texturing, and rendering, as well as proficiency in a range of software tools, I'm well-equipped to embark on new artistic and technological endeavors. If you're seeking an artist with a passion for the fantasy genre, creature modeling, character creation, and a growing expertise in software development, I'm here and eager to bring your imaginative visions to life.
